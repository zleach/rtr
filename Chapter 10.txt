Emily's resolve is thoroughly tested as she undergoes a psychological evaluation with Dr. Michaels, a probing session designed to determine her fitness to invoke the state's controversial Right to Retribution. As she navigates the depths of her past, revealing layers of childhood trauma and the shadows that have followed her into adulthood, Emily confronts the intertwining of her grief and her desire for justice. With Dr. Michaels as her steady yet insistent guide, she peers into the mirror of her own psyche, searching for clarity. After the session, she is drawn into the dark echoes of shared regret online, leading her to question the true cost of vengeance. As the chapter closes, the boundaries between Emily's inner turmoil and the external world blur, leaving her at the precipice of a decision that could echo with irreversible consequences.
