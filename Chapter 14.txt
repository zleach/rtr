In a world where personal boundaries are easily trespassed and the sanctity of home is violently disrupted, Emily finds herself grappling with the unsettling aftermath of a harrowing incident at her abode. A simple visit to her father's ranch morphs into a stark confrontation with the necessity of self-defense as she is introduced to the cold steel of protection. As the rugged Texas landscape bears silent witness, Emily must navigate the complicated weave of family ties, long-standing traditions, and the unspoken expectations of what it means to stand one's ground. Amidst memories of a wilder youth and the looming threat to her present, Emily faces the daunting challenge of embracing a power she never desired, drawing lines she isn't sure she wants to defend.
