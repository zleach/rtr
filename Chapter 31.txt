In the aftermath of a devastating tornado that has stripped Emily of her childhood home and legacy, the Rawlings Ranch lies in ruin. As the community rallies to piece together the fragments of what once was, a shadow from Emily's tumultuous past re-emerges, weaving a new thread of tension into the fabric of the day's sorrow. David Rutledge, the man tried for her sister's murder, descends upon the disaster under the guise of political goodwill, igniting a firestorm of unresolved anger and setting the stage for an unexpected confrontation. With her father and the remnants of her once unshakable foundation surrounding her, Emily must navigate the complexity of loss, politics, and the pursuit of a justice that feels increasingly elusive.
