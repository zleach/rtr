Emily embarks on an emotionally charged journey through the remnants of her late sister's life, preserved within a dusty box filled with memories and secrets. As she unveils trinkets and mementos of Hannah's existence—ranging from childhood keepsakes to the more troubling artifacts of her adulthood—Emily is confronted with an unexpected letter that will challenge her understanding of forgiveness and the indelible bonds of sisterhood. In this solemn exploration of the personal items left behind, Emily is drawn closer to the mysteries that enshroud her sister's untimely demise, uncovering a narrative that is as heart-rending as it is telling, leading her to reckon with a past fraught with love, loss, and the relentless pursuit of truth.
