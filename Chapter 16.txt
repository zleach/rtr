Tensions boil over into violence, leaving behind a trail of consequences and heartfelt admissions. When Emily rushes to the hospital to confront her husband Wade after a workplace altercation spirals out of control, she uncovers layers of loyalty, misguided heroism, and the raw edges of personal and political battles. They grapple with the consequences of their choices, seeking solace in the promise of a simpler, quieter moment in the future. But beneath the surface, there is an unspoken recognition of the disturbing undercurrents in their lives, hinting at deeper conflicts and the unsettling awareness that some battles may be unavoidable.
