In the haunting twilight of their coastal retreat, Emily and Wade's final day unfolds with an unexpected encounter that leads to an invitation to a New Year's Eve party hosted by local residents, Jeannie and Troy. As the night unravels, Emily is swept into a tumultuous sea of emotions, struggling to keep afloat amidst the memories of her sister and the dark shadow cast by David Rutledge's trial. As the countdown to midnight echoes with revelry and resolutions, Emily finds herself on a precipice, her cries lost amidst the fireworks and waves, only to be drawn back by Wade's unwavering support. A glimpse into their intertwined journey reveals a landscape of love and resilience, but also the harrowing depths of a soul yearning for retribution and a respite from its burdening past.
